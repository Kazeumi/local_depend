Haroopad ShortCuts
======================

`Shift Ctrl D`: Insert Current Date

`Ctrl + [` & `Ctrl + ]`: 【, 】

`Ctrl + Z`: Undo
`Ctrl + Shift + Z`: Un-undo

`Alt + left/right`: home/end
`Alt + up`: Zoom in

`Alt + down`: Zoom out

`Ctrl + Shift + H`: Open/Close quick Md ref

`Ctrl + Shift + G`: Show/Hide line number
