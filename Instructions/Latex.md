* `\,` or `\hspace{1.5 mm}`: insert space
 
* `\begin{array}{c}` \<elements> `\end{array}`
    * `\\` between elements: new row
    * `&` between elements: new column
    * `\left(`, `\right)`: round braces
    * `\left[`, `\right]`: square braces
