## Consonant parameters

### voicing
聲帶是否振動

### place of articulation
- bilabial
- labiodental
- interdental
- alveolar: t, d, s
- palatal(硬齶）
- velar: k, g
- glottal: h, bat, 鴨（台語）

### manner of articulation
- stop: p, k, t
- fricative: s
- affricate: stop + fricative
- nasal: m, n
- liquid
- glide
- tap

### voicing > place > manner
b: Voiced, bilabial, stop


### Examples
台語
- 肉 b: voiced
- 爸 p: unvoiced, unaspirated
- 打 p^h^: unvoiced, unaspirated
